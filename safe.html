Ocean Poem

the new collection by

George Murray

Murray's compositions are delicate and evocative vignettes. They breathe harsh winter, cold Maine beaches, the tranquility of quiet friendship. They echo with remorse, with patience, with awe for an endless and cruel sea. They speak of autumn dancing in a church auditorium, of I-495 to the Cape, of thinking silently about one you really loved, years and years since you've spoken to them.PRDNJY.CO
